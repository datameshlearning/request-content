What Data Mesh Content Do You Want to See?

This is an idea that was sparked from a conversation with Robert Sahlin (who was the genesis for this community). Robert was saying he wanted more content around a few topics specifically and most of the content was either aspirational - oh what adventures ROI you'll have - or what an end state deployed data mesh looked like. This wasn't quite the level of underpants gnomes but there are some missing gaps out there about how to go from no mesh to a mesh.

We want to create a system for people to be able to put out into the world the kind of content they want to see. If others are listening and can deliver that content, we all benefit. And it means the signal to noise ratio from vendors being the main source of content is significantly better than in many industries. This is modeled after what has worked - and what hasn't, mainly leaving it to the vendors - in a number of open source technology communities.

We have created a submission form for requesting content and also have a summarized high-level wish list. As part of the submission process you can provide more information and we will put it into the raw content request Google sheet as well if you prefer; that way, it won't be a summation of your words.

As described above, this is a bit of a crazy idea so feel free to help us shape it into something that works well. We are hoping to put in a voting system as well so people can throw their support behind content requests.

Also, once we get to a decent critical mass, we plan to start doing questions of the week where we will vote on a topic and then have a set time where we create a new room to discuss that topic which should allow for some great focused discussions. Feedback very welcome.
